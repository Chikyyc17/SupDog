OUR STORY

Born from a passion for bold flavors and street food culture, SupDog brings an attitude to classic American favorites. Located in the heart of Grapevine, TX, we're not just serving hot dogs - we're crafting flavor experiences that challenge the ordinary.

Every SupDog creation starts with premium ingredients, from our signature beef franks to house-made sauces and fresh toppings. Our nachos are loaded with personality, and our sides are anything but basic.

We believe great food should be fun, accessible, and memorable. That's why we've created a space where food lovers can gather, share, and enjoy bold flavors without pretense.

Follow our journey on Instagram to see behind-the-scenes, new menu drops, and meet the team that makes it all happen!
